List of Compounds

From the List of Compounds the prevalence of the compounds in the AlphaFill databank can be retrieved. This table presents the compounds with their analogue and the number of AlphaFold models to which the compound was transplanted (# of structures), plus the number on how often the compound was transplanted in total (# of transplants). By clicking on a compound, a list of all AlphaFill entries containing that compound is displayed.
